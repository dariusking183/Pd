📁 Reports System

Submit detailed incident reports with evidence.

Must include full explanation and context.

False reporting leads to disciplinary action.

🚨 Critical Incidents

For nuking, exploiting, mass abuse, or server threats.

Automatically escalated to High Command.

If confirmed → Executive Officers review case.

👑 Chain of Command

Step 1: High Command reviews case

Step 2: Executive Officers analyze

Step 3: Final decision issued

Denied cases are permanently logged.

🚓 Patrol System

Officers must log patrol activity.

Used for performance tracking and promotions.

Inactive members may be demoted.

🧾 Case Files

All investigations are stored here.

Includes evidence, logs, and final rulings.

Only High Command and above can modify cases.

🛡 Security Hub

Monitors server threats in real-time.

Detects spam, nuking attempts, and mass actions.

Alerts High Command instantly on suspicious activity.

Low Ranks → Middle Ranks → High Command → Executive Officers

Executive Officers = final authority
